Martial Arts Disciplines

Our academy offers a variety of martial arts classes, including Jiu-jitsu, Karate, Judo, and Muay Thai, delivered by experienced instructors.

Jiu-jitsu

A technique-focused discipline centred on grappling and control. Ideal for building confidence and practical self-defence skills.

  • Beginner-friendly approach
  • Focus on technique and control
  • Suitable for teenagers and adults

Karate

A structured martial art that develops discipline, striking techniques, and mental focus while improving overall fitness.

  • Emphasis on discipline and forms
  • Fundamental striking techniques
  • Suitable for all experience levels

Judo

A dynamic discipline based on throws, balance, and movement, helping to improve coordination and physical control.

  • Focus on throws and balance
  • Safe and controlled training
  • Enhances cardiovascular fitness

Muay Thai

A high-intensity striking art that improves endurance, strength, and full-body conditioning.

  • Striking and conditioning
  • High calorie-burning workouts
  • Builds stamina and resilience

Our facilities include a fully equipped gym, a matted training area, sauna, steam room, and modern changing facilities.
